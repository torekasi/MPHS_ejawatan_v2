# User Guide: MPHS Job Application System

## 1. Overview
Welcome to the Majlis Perbandaran Hulu Selangor (MPHS) Job Application System. This system allows candidates to apply for job vacancies, track their application status, and manage their personal information securely.

## 2. How to Apply

### Step 1: Access the Application Form
Navigate to the main application page (`index.php` or `job-application-full.php`). You will be presented with the comprehensive application form.

### Step 2: Complete the Form Sections
The form is divided into several key sections:

1.  **Agreement**: Read and accept the terms and declarations.
2.  **Upload Documents**: Upload required identification documents (IC, Birth Certificate, Passport Photo, etc.).
    *   *Note*: Supported formats are JPG, PNG, PDF. Max size 5MB.
3.  **Personal Information**: Fill in your name, IC, contact details, and address.
    *   *Tip*: Use the "Same as permanent address" checkbox if your correspondence address is the same.
4.  **Health & OKU**: Declare any health conditions or OKU status.
5.  **Education & Skills**:
    *   **SPM**: Enter your SPM results.
    *   **Education**: Add your higher education details.
    *   **Skills**: List your language and computer proficiencies.
6.  **Experience**: Add your past work experience.
7.  **Declarations**: Answer the mandatory self-declaration questions (e.g., criminal record, bankruptcy).
8.  **References**: Provide two referees who are not family members.

### Step 3: Submit
Review all your entries. Click the "Submit Application" button. You will receive a confirmation message and an Application Reference Number upon success.

## 3. Checking Application Status

You can check the status of your application at any time.

1.  Go to the **Check Status** page (`semak-status.php`).
2.  Enter your **IC Number**.
3.  Click **Check**.
4.  Your current application status (e.g., `PENDING`, `TEST_INTERVIEW`) will be displayed.

## 4. Editing Your Application

If you need to update your information/documents:

1.  Go to the **Check Status** page.
2.  Enter your IC and view your application.
3.  Click the **Edit** button (if available).
4.  You may be asked to verify your identity via email or a security token.
5.  Once verified, you can modify your details and resubmit.

## 5. FAQ / Troubleshooting

**Q: My file upload failed.**
A: Ensure your file is under 5MB and is in JPG, PNG, or PDF format.

**Q: I cannot submit the form.**
A: Check for any red error messages next to the fields. Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are filled.

**Q: The page timed out.**
A: For security, the form has a time limit (default 30 mins). Please refresh and try again if you see a timeout message.
